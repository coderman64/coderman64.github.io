The Path

Welcome to The Path!

In a world of black and white, you must forge your road ahead through a treacherous jungle of dots. Three different powerups will help you along your way, but beware! Or you might just find your path has come to an end.

Features:

  • Infinite, progressively more difficult gameplay.
  • 3 distinct types of powerups.
  • Smart scaling for any display.
  • Offline gameplay.
  • Pure awesomeness!

What is it?

The Path is a game that I first created around 2017 for Android phones and Samsung smartwatches. While it has had only modest success on Google Play, it has been downloaded over 9,000 times on the Galaxy Watch Store and counting.

Originally, I created the app around its pronounced visual style and the idea of an "infinite runner" type game that only allowed you to turn at right angles. After building a quick mockup of the game, I used technologies such as Apache Cordova to build it into a full app featuring powerups to shake up the gameplay a bit, as well as a healthy amount of polish.
